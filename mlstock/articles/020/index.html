Machine Learning for Stock Markets

Machine Learning Application for Stock Market Prices Prediction



Autori

C. Ugwu, OnwuachuUzochukwu C

Anno

2014

In breve

Implemetazione di un software di analisi dei mercati in Java. L’articolo copre sia gli aspetti implementativi, utilizzando la programmazione ad oggetti, sia gli aspetti sperimentali tramite l’analisi dei risultati.

Metodi

I valori delle 14 variabili vengono normalizzati per ottenere valori binari, rappresentanti l’attivazione dei neuroni di una Neural Network. Vengono poi provate differenti strutture di reti neurali, modificando il numero di neuroni appartenenti allo hidden layer.

Dataset

Mercato azionario nigeriano, periodo Gennaio 2008 - Marzo 2013. I seguenti valori sono presi in considerazione:

  • opening price of day i-1
  • opening price of day i-2
  • daily high price of day i-1
  • daily high price of day i-2
  • daily low price of day i-1
  • daily low price of day i-2
  • closing price of day i-1
  • closing price of day i-2
  • trading volume of day i-1
  • trading volume of day i-2
  • gross domestic product of year i-1
  • gross domestic product of year i-2
  • inflation rate of year i-1
  • inflation rate of year i-2

Risultati

The developed application does its predication of stock market prices with minimum amount of error. It was also observed from the experimental results that optimal prediction can be achieved by varying the number of the hidden neuron. The initialization scheme may be improved by estimating weights between input nodes and hidden nodes, instead of random initialization. Enrichment of more relevant inputs such as fundamental data and technical data from derivative markets may improve the predictability of the network. Applying Neural Network back propagation learning algorithm in training data for stock prediction has been shown in this paper to be an efficient tool in developing applications for stock prediction.
